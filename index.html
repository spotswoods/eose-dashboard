02 · INVESTMENT THESIS

Why Eos, in three steps

A long-duration storage company crossing from R&D into commercial scale, now with a Cerberus-anchored IPP vehicle (Frontier Power USA) that solves the LDES project-finance bottleneck.

01 · MARKET

Long-duration is the missing piece of the grid.

As renewables exceed 30% of grid generation, 4–12+ hour storage becomes structurally needed. Lithium-ion wins 1–4 hours but degrades on duration economics. The DOE estimates 225–460 GW of long-duration storage will be needed in the US alone by 2050. Eos' commercial pipeline already sits at $24.3B (+56% YoY).

02 · MOAT

Zinc-bromide chemistry & 100% US supply.

Aqueous Znyth® is non-flammable, uses non-CRM zinc + bromine, and qualifies for the 45X manufacturing PTC (Eos has $21.4M of grant receivable on the balance sheet). Manufactured at scale in Pittsburgh, PA — the only US-listed LDES manufacturer at meaningful commercial volume.

03 · INFLECTION

Line 2 powers on; Frontier unlocks the demand side.

Line 2 installation at Thorn Hill is underway with initial production targeted for end of Q2'26. Frontier Power USA (Cerberus JV, $100M + ~$150M Eos, 2 GWh firm reservation) gives Eos a contracted off-take vehicle wrapped by Ariel Green TPI — making Z3 output financeable for project lenders for the first time.

03 · FRONTIER POWER USA

The Cerberus joint venture, in plain English

Stand-alone IPP formed May 13, 2026 to build, own, and operate LDES projects deploying Eos' Z3. Combines Eos tech + Cerberus capital + Ariel Green Tech Performance Insurance.
