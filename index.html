      03 · FRONTIER POWER PLATFORM

      Two Frontier entities — UK (since 2009, ~11 GWh in Ofgem) + USA (new May 2026)

      Frontier Power Ltd. (UK) is a 2009-vintage UK developer with a 5 GWh Eos framework, a booked 228 MWh first order (Oct 2025), and ~11 GWh advancing through Ofgem's Cap & Floor scheme. Frontier Power USA is a separate Cerberus-backed IPP formed May 13, 2026 with a 2 GWh anchor reservation. They share a brand family and a Cerberus connection but are distinct vehicles. Coverage often conflates them — the "11 GWh" figure belongs to the UK entity.
